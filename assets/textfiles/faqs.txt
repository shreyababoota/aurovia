
1. What is the Harit Index?
    Ans. The Harit Index is a 0-100 score that reflects the environmental impact of a product based on its manufacturing emissions, packaging, logistics, and certifications.

2. How is the score calculated?
    Ans. The score is computed using verified data from suppliers, life-cycle assessments, and 	AI-based estimations when full data is not available.

3. Where can I see the Harit Index on products?
    Ans. It appears as a badge or label on product pages and can be filtered in search and 	category results.

4. How do I know the score is accurate?
   Ans. All scores are backed by data submitted by vendors and validated by Aurovia's 	scoring engine using standard sustainability benchmarks.

5. Can I trust products with a high Harit Index?
   Ans. Yes. A high score indicates that the product is more sustainable compared to others in 	its category.

6. What does a low Harit Index mean?
   Ans. A low score suggests higher emissions, non-recyclable packaging, or lack of sustainability certifications.

7. Can I filter products by Harit Index?
   Ans. Yes. Our app provides sorting and filtering options to help you choose eco-friendly alternatives.

8. How does this help the environment?
   Ans.  By choosing high-score products, you help reduce your carbon footprint and support sustainable practices.

9. Do I get rewarded for eco-friendly shopping?
   Ans. Yes. You can earn GreenPoints for buying sustainable products, which can be redeemed for rewards or donations.

10. Can I track my personal sustainability impact?
   Ans. Yes. Your My Green Impact dashboard shows total CO? saved and greener alternatives you've chosen.

11. How do I see my product's Harit Index?
   Ans. You can log into the vendor dashboard and view the Harit score along with its 	contributing factors.

12. What can I do to improve my score?
   Ans. The dashboard provides actionable insights, such as using recyclable packaging or choosing low-emission shipping methods.

13. What are the benefits of improving my Harit Index?
   Ans. Products with higher scores get featured in the Eco Leaderboard and receive more 	visibility on the platform.

14. What data do I need to provide?
   Ans. Basic product details, packaging type, logistics info, energy consumption, and any 	sustainability certifications.
   
15. What if I dont have complete data?
   Ans. Our AI Estimator can predict scores based on available industry data and similar 	product categories.
